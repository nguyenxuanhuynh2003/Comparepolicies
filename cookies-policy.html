Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how ComparePolicies.uk ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website https://comparepolicies.uk ("Website"). It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control how we use them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide website owners with useful information such as site usage statistics.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (stored until deleted or expired).

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

a. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our Website to function properly. They enable core features such as page navigation, security, and accessibility.

Examples:

  • Remembering your cookie preferences
  • Securing form submissions

b. Analytical / Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Website by collecting information anonymously (e.g., which pages are most visited, how long users stay).

Examples:

  • Google Analytics or similar tools

c. Functionality Cookies

These allow the Website to remember choices you make, such as your region or preferences, to provide enhanced and personalized features.

d. Advertising / Third-Party Cookies

We may partner with third-party services that use cookies to display relevant advertisements or track engagement with links or affiliate offers.

Examples:

  • Google Ads or affiliate tracking platforms

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third parties that provide services to us or display content through our Website.
We do not control these cookies and recommend checking their respective cookie policies for more information.

Common examples include:

  • Google Analytics (website performance tracking)
  • YouTube / Vimeo (if embedding videos)
  • Affiliate networks or comparison tools

4. How to Manage or Delete Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Cookie Banner: When you first visit ComparePolicies.uk, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies.
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to delete or block cookies.
    • Chrome
    • Firefox
    • Safari
    • Edge

Please note that disabling cookies may affect how our Website functions.

5. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
The updated version will be posted on this page with the "Last updated" date revised.
